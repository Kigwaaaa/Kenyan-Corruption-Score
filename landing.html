About Kenya Finance Watch

Kenya Finance Watch is a comprehensive platform dedicated to monitoring and analyzing financial irregularities in the Kenyan Finance Bill 2024-2025. Our mission is to promote transparency, accountability, and informed public discourse about government spending.

Our Methodology

The Corruption Index is calculated using a sophisticated formula that takes into account multiple factors:

Corruption Index = (w1 × Financial Irregularities) + (w2 × Transparency Score) + (w3 × Historical Corruption Risk)

Where:

  • w1 = 0.5 (Weight for Financial Irregularities)
  • w2 = 0.3 (Weight for Transparency Score)
  • w3 = 0.2 (Weight for Historical Corruption Risk)

Each ministry is evaluated based on:

  • Budget vs. Actual Expenditure Discrepancies
  • Documentation and Reporting Quality
  • Historical Patterns of Financial Management
  • Public Procurement Transparency

Why This Matters

Transparency

By making financial data accessible and understandable, we empower citizens to hold their government accountable.

Accountability

Our platform helps identify patterns of financial irregularities, enabling better oversight and corrective action.

Informed Decision Making

Citizens and stakeholders can make better decisions when they have clear insights into government spending.
